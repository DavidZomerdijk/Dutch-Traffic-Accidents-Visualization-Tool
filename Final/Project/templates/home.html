Dutch Traffic Accidents


This visualization shows the traffic accidents in the 12 provinces of the Netherlands
  • In the map on left the accidents per capita are depicted. Hovering over the provinces shows the exact # accidents and accidents per capita on the top right of the map.
  • In the barchart the accidents per capita are depicted in descending order to help you find the most dangerous province. By using the buttons underneath you can change the the unit to # accidents per province.
  • It is possible to filter on the year, weather conditions and time
  • Click on a province to see the accidents in a province
    • On the top right there is an option to find the most dangerous locations
    • Cluster the accidents by selecting the meters
